Introduction¶

Baseball is the best sport out there. There are lot of players who get a chance to play in the major league right after the highschool . But there are players who consider joining the college after the highschool. Do they have to be careful in picking their college ?. Getting into good college have any positive impact in their baseballcareer ?.
To answer this questions i am going to split the players into 4 groups

  • Players from the top colleges

  • players from the colleges other than the top colleges

  • players who didn't attend the college

  • players who attend the college

    • To measure how successful their career is i use two attributes salary and awards. why salary and awards? salary: It varies from player to player. Based on thier performance their salary is decided. If the player performs well in the matches he will get a good salary.

    salary of the baseball is decide using the salary arbitaration process and using the free agency.

    Salary arbitaration process : Baseball's arbitration process is based on the "last best offer" principle. Under this system, the parties bargain to impass on the topic of the salary. Once it is determined that continued negotiations would be fruitless, both management and player submit one proposed salary figure to the arbitrator. The arbitarator then holds hearings, alloting one and one half for each side to present their evidence. The arbitrator must choose one figure or the other, either the player's or management's and award the player that salary for the next season. The creteria on which the arbitarator bases the decision are as follows:

  • The quality of the player's contribution to the club during the past season

  • length and the consistence of the player's career performance

  • The player's past compensation

  • comparative baseball salaries
    • Awards : Awards are for the players who performed exceptionally well.
    My aim here is to compare the salary and awards of the 4 groups to see the college education have any impact on the career.

    def calculate_percentage(x):
    ''' To calculate the percentage of players who received awards
    It counts the total players in the group and the number of players who received the awards 
    Divide the number of player with the awards by total count of players and multiply by 100'''
    total_count = x['playerID'].count()
    player_with_awards = x['awardID'].count()
    answer = float(player_with_awards* 100.0 / total_count)
    return answer

    Conclusion¶

    By this analysis we can't conclude that players who haven't attended any colleges are being successful in the career. They may be getting high salary and awards due to lot of different factors so we can't compare the players who have attend the college to the players who haven't attend college.

    But difference here between the players who from the top college and the players from the other college is cleary visible in every chart. They are splited based on the number of baseball players from each college . Those college who have 40 players as their alumini is grouped into top college and the remaining players are grouped into other college players. top college players has the high mean for the salary and the percentages of the awards gotten by the players also high comparing to the players from the other college. It doesn't mean you can't be successful when you study in the other colleges.But the probability is low comparing with the others.

    Comparing the non college player with the players from the top college , non college players has a high mean in salary and the noticable upper hand in the percentage of awards they got. It conveys that the college education is not important for successfull baseball career. Regardless of eduaction players with the knack can have a successful career in the field. But who are all opting out for the college education should be careful in selecting their college as it may change their baseball career a bit.
